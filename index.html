The Artist

From the Streets
to the Sacred

Dell Mac is a Christian artist from Maryland, born August 28, 1997. His music is defined by angelic vocals and smooth melodies — guitar instrumentals woven through clean 808s — creating a sound that is both intimate and cinematic.

His journey is one of transformation. Like many artists, Dell Mac began in secular music before experiencing a profound shift in faith that redirected every gift he had. He describes this not simply as a genre change, but as a divine calling — a renewed sense of purpose to create music that uplifts, inspires, and points others to Christ.

His output has grown steadily: collaborative EPs, a string of deeply personal singles, and his 2026 album MAC all signal an artist at the height of his creative and spiritual vision. The Gospel Coalition named him one of 15 Christian artists to watch in 2025.
